2. Types of Personal Information We Collect

We may collect and process the following categories of personal information depending on how you interact with our services:

  • Health & Fitness Data: Including heart rate, step count, sleep patterns, calories burned, physical activity duration, weight trends, and biometric measurements. Collected via device sensors or integrations with platforms like Google Fit or Apple Health.
  • Location Data: GPS and network-based location data to map routes, show fitness insights, or enable features such as step tracking. Users are prompted to grant location permission explicitly.
  • Photos and Camera Access: When you use features like progress photo uploads or scan QR codes, we may request access to your camera and photo gallery. This access is strictly used for intended in-app functions and never stored without your consent.
  • Contacts Information: Only if you choose to invite friends or share achievements with your contacts. We never access or store contact information without explicit permission.
  • Personal Identifiers: Including your name, profile photo, gender, age, email address, date of birth, and account credentials used during signup or profile creation.
  • Device and App Usage Information: Includes device model, operating system, language settings, crash reports, and in-app activity logs. Helps us improve performance and diagnose issues.
  • Transaction and Payment Data: Such as subscription type, renewal status, transaction ID, and purchase history (via App Store or Google Play).

3. How We Use Your Information

We collect and use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • To deliver core functionalities such as activity tracking, fitness insights, health goals, and visual reports.

  • To personalize your experience by tailoring features, reminders, or content based on your usage.
  • To fulfill subscription management, billing support, and customer inquiries.
  • To comply with legal, regulatory, or security obligations.
  • To send occasional product updates, promotions, or health-related tips, with opt-out options.
