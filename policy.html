1. Introduction

FitTrack Pty Ltd ("FitTrack", "we", "us" or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and disclose your personal information when you use our Websites, Apps, and Services. By using our products, you consent to the collection, holding, use, and disclosure of your personal data as outlined in this policy.

2. Information We Collect and Hold

FitTrack collects personal information in various ways, including when you:

  • Use our website, social media pages, or internal platforms ("Website").
  • Use our mobile or tablet applications ("Apps").
  • Interact with us via customer service, surveys, or support inquiries.
  • Are present in locations where our security systems operate.

2.1 Categories of Personal Information We Collect

  • Personal Identifiers: Name, username, profile picture, date of birth, contact details.
  • Health & Activity Data: Steps count, heart rate, workout sessions, calories burned.
  • Location Data: GPS-based real-time tracking for fitness route mapping.
  • Device & Usage Information: Device model, operating system, app interactions, crash reports.
  • Transaction Information: Purchase history, subscription details, loyalty rewards.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data for the following purposes:

  • Providing fitness tracking and insights.
  • Improving user experience and app functionality.
  • Sending notifications, recommendations, and promotions.
  • Ensuring security and fraud prevention.
  • Complying with legal and regulatory obligations.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. However, we may share data with:

  • Service providers for app functionality (e.g., cloud storage, analytics).
  • Third-party integrations (e.g., Apple Health, Google Fit).
  • Legal authorities if required by law.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or loss. However, no system is completely secure, and we encourage you to use strong passwords.

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:
